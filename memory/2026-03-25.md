### 3. Key Design Decisions

**Why 50% Risk Per Trade?**
- Traditional risk management (20-30% stops) leaves money on the table in crash/bounce markets
- Capitulation bounces can be 30-50%+; you either commit or miss it
- Mean reversion + momentum chasing both require conviction

**Why Multiple Concurrent Positions?**
- Single-pair strategies (S1-S3) leave capital idle between trades
- Gainers/Losers feed is high-turnover; need to be in 3-4 trades at once to capture volume
- Compounding accelerates with more trades (if execution is clean)

**Why Coinbase Big Gainers/Losers?**
- Real market data, not synthetic/backtest
- High-volatility tokens (pump candidates)
- Clear entry signals (RSI, volume, momentum confirmation)
- Smaller latency advantage vs. fancy ML (first 60s of each update wins)

### 5. Risk Warnings Documented

1. **50% per-trade is EXTREME** — If both bots hit -50% same day, portfolio halves
2. **Feed latency matters** — Real alpha in first 60 seconds of Gainers/Losers refresh
3. **Slippage & fees bleed** — Coinbase 0.5-1% per trade + 2-5% slippage = 1-2% cost per trade
4. **Rug pulls & halts** — Altcoins can disappear; hard stops may not fill
5. **Drawdown trigger:** 35% max portfolio drawdown → liquidate all immediately
